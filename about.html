About Us

We are Colonial High School's official coding club, based in Orlando, FL. If you're in CHS and you're interested in competitive coding, competitive hacking, and more, then this is the place for you!

We participate in Lockheed Martin's CodeQuest and CyberQuest every year, as well as more local competitions, such as OTC's Capture The Flag.
